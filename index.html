I am an undergraduate student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (expected to graduate in Jun 2023), studying Bachelor of Science (Bsc) in Data Science and Technology and Bachelor of Science (Bsc) in Computer Science. My final-year thesis is supervised by Professor Shueng-Han Gary Chan.

My research interest lies in the intersection of machine learning and computer vision, specifically with the following focuses:

  • 3D reconstruction with Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF).
  • Designing computation/time-efficient operators and architectures of neural networks.
  • Knowledge Distillation for model compression and acceleration.

News: I will join Professor Chi-Keung Tang's research group for the Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST in Sep 2023.
