Welcome to my homepage!🤗

I am an M.Phil. student in the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and co-supervision of Prof. Yu-Wing Tai. Previously, I served as a research intern in the Media Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia from June 2024 to December 2024, where I was privileged to work with Dr. Xiao Li and Dr. Jinglu Wang. Prior to my graduate studies, I earned a Bachelor of Science degree from HKUST, double-majoring in Data Science and Technology and in Computer Science. I completed my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Prof. Shueng-Han Gary Chan, in cooperation with Dr. Jierun Chen.

My research interests primarily lie in the intersection of deep learning and computer vision, with particular concentrations on areas such as:

  • 2D/3D content generation with Latent Diffusion Models (LDM).
  • 3D reconstruction with Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) and 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS).
  • Vision-language reasoning with Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLM).
  • Neural network compression techniques, e.g. Knowledge Distillation (KD).
