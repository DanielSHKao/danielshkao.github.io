I am a new M.Phil. student at HKUST CSE Department, advised by Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai. I graduated from HKUST with B.Sc. in Data Science and Technology and B.Sc. in Computer Science, where I was lucky to join Prof. Shueng-Han Gary Chan's group and cooperate with Dr. Jierun Chen for my final-year thesis.

My research interest lies in the intersection of machine learning and computer vision, specifically with the following focuses:

  • 3D reconstruction with Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF).
  • Designing computation/time-efficient operators and architectures of neural networks.
  • Knowledge Distillation for model compression and acceleration.
